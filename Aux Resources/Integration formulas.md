# Sine
$$
\int \sin(u) \,du = -\cos(u) + C  
$$

# Cosine
$$ 
\int \cos(u) \,du = \sin(u) + C
$$

# Exponential
$$
\int e^u \,du = e^u + C
$$

# Logarithm
$$
\int \ln(u) \,du = u\ln(u) - u + C
$$ 

# Polynomial
$$
\int_a^b kx^n \,dx = \frac{k}{n+1}(b^{n+1} - a^{n+1}) 
$$

# Parts
$$
\int u\,dv = uv - \int v\,du
$$

# Substitution  
$$
\int f(g(x))g'(x) \,dx = \int f(u) \,du
$$
